About

  • IEEE Computer Society
    The IEEE Computer Society is the world's leading membership organization dedicated to computer science and technology. Serving more than 60,000 members, the IEEE Computer Society is the trusted information, networking, and career-development source for a global community of technology leaders that includes researchers, educators, software engineers, IT professionals, employers, and students.

  • Computer Society, India Council
    IEEE Computer Society - India Council Chapter groups IEEE members who share a common interest in the field of computers and its applications.We help in the advancement of theory, practice, and application of computer and information processing science and technology. CS-IC sponsors various workshops and conferences across the country. We offer our members a collaborative environment for creativity and productivity as well as the resources to advance their knowledge and professional training.The Computer Society maintains volunteer boards in six program areas: education, membership, professional activities, publications, standards, and technical and conference activities.
